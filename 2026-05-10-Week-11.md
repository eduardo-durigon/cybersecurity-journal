### 💡 Key Concepts

**The Role of an IT Specialist**

* IT support is not only about fixing computers, but also about communication, organization, and problem-solving
* Troubleshooting requires a logical and structured process
* Documentation and consistency are important skills in technical support

**Troubleshooting Methodology**

This week introduced the standard troubleshooting process used in IT environments:

1. Identify the problem
2. Establish a theory of probable cause
3. Test the theory
4. Create a plan of action
5. Verify full system functionality
6. Document findings and solutions

Understanding this process helped reinforce the importance of approaching technical problems calmly and methodically instead of guessing solutions.
